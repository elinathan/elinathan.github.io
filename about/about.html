About me

Hi! I'm a graphic designer and photographer from Princeton, NJ, currently studying at UPenn. I specialize in designing and developing visual identities, brand guidelines, and publications.

I’m currently on the creative team in Muse, the biggest marketing club at the Wharton School and UPenn developing B2B brand guidelines for rePurpose. I also work on the design team at Penn Appétit, a student-run food magazine.

In 2018, after the tragic school shooting that February, I was asked to create graphics for the March for Our Lives in Parkland, Florida. The designs I created were utilized and seen extensively, including on national television. In recognition of my work, JP Morgan Chase subsequently invited me to their global headquarters in New York City, where I met with and was inspired by many top artists working in their creative team.

My work has been exhibited in the Montclair Art Museum and The Arts Council of Princeton. It has also been seen on many national news networks including CNN, MSNBC, and HLN.
